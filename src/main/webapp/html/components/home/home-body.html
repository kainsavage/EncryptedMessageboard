In order to do anything useful here, you will first need to generate a public key (allowing others to send you encrypted messages) and a private key (in order to decrypt those encrypted messages).

This will require you to register.

It is important to note that while we require a valid user registration, we do not require any personal information. Other sites would have you fill the following out, at least:

  • Your name
  • Your email
The only bits of information we require are non-private ones:
  • Your public key (which will be built afterwards)
  • A username/nickname
  • A password for this login

There are many reasons why we decided to keep the required data to a minimum, but they required a lot of explanation and can become really rather technical. You can, and are advised, to read about them on the FAQ.
